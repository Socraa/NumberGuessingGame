Guess The Number From 1-100

How to Play "Guess the Random Number"

            1. The game will randomly select a number between 1 and 100.

            2. You have 10 attempts to guess the correct number.

            3. After each guess, you will be told if your guess is too high, too low, or correct.

            4. If you guess the correct number within 10 attempts, you win!              

            5. If you do not guess the number within 10 attempts, you lose and the correct number will be revealed.
            
            6. Have fun and good luck!
